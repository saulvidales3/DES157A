Overview

The purpose of this project was to challenge traditional city planning. I tried to incorporate interactive pieces, such as the labels that appear when you hover over the leaves and an interactive 3D city model. There are also architectural drawings that accompany the site's interactive pieces to help bring the overall flow of the green city to life.

Process

For this project, I wanted to visualize a city that prioritized plants. I put a carousel with “green is law” because I wanted users to get a grasp of the city's belief systems without outwardly saying what my project was about. I created the interactive 3D green city model using a library from a-frame.io. I made the architectural drawings in Photoshop and Illustrator. I decided to do this to highlight housing, transportation, and infrastructure so users could understand how this city operates. Overall, I tried to show and not tell in my green city project by incorporating multiple elements and letting users understand the overall flow of the website.
