Sunny Day

It is a sunny day in San Francisco. This a nice pathway in Salesforce Park that provides seating on the left for visitors and has an abundance of natural elements such as plants and water fountains.

Night Underpass

This underpass around segundo connects UC Davis' campus to Davis and is used by students to leave and enter campus. On the other side of this underpass if you walk further you could find the U-mall.

Trees & Concrete

This pathway is hidden behind Wellman hall and connects students with nature by having the surroundings of Wellman hall adorned with vegetation.

Bikes & People

This path is short but treacherous during passing period. Bikes, scooters, cars, and pedestrains have to work together to avoid crashing into each other.

Roadway

This pathway is alongside a road and could feel unsafe when vehicles travel at high speeds. Overall this pathway depicts the various forms of transportation such as transportation by foot, car, or public transportation.
